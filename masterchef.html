paruthi halwa

ingredients

  • cotton milk\cotton seeds
  • blackberry\palm jaggery
  • cashews
  • coconut oil
  • crushed cardamom

preparation methods

  • wash the cotton seeds 3 to 4 times to remove the impurities.then soak it for overnight or 8 hrs
  • grind the soaked cotton seeds with 2 cups of water into the paste.again grind the cotton solids with the 2 cup of water extract the milk continue to do until you have try cotton solids
  • make the blackberry\palm jaggery syrup with 3/4 cups of waterand keep aside.roast the cashews in the coconut oil and keep aside
  • bring to boil,reduce the flame and stir continuously to avoid burnt at the bottom
  • if the mixture is become too thick and reached the halwa consistency means,add the coconut oil little by little and mix well and cook until the mixture turns into the dark color
  • add the cardamom and roasted cashews,one by one,mix well and remove from the flame
  • cool little and ready to serve
